About Me

I'm Elvis Kibet Ngetich, a 20-year-old self-taught developer from Kericho, Kenya. I live in a humble mud house, help my mom on our small tea farm, and build full-stack web apps, APIs, and ethical hacking tools using only a smartphone and Termux.

My dream is to study software engineering and AI at Yale or MIT. I’m building a faceless content brand—elvo_alpha—to inspire others like me across Africa. I believe in turning struggle into legacy, and I’m documenting every step of my journey to show what’s possible when you refuse to give up.
